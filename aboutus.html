ABOUT US

Welcome to a world where ancient wisdom is infused with modern aesthetic.

Forest Essentials is an authentic, traditional skincare brand with its foundations in the ancient science of Ayurveda. A pioneer of Luxurious Ayurveda, today it is the quintessential Indian beauty brand that combines ancient beauty rituals with a modern aesthetic.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

If You Cannot Eat It, Do Not Use It On Your Skin

Beauty products made of living substances such as plants or their extracts contain the vibratory energy of life. These ingredients are perfect foods for the skin, naturally purifying, nutritive, and balancing.
